Welcome

Thanks for using Parking Pal. This application is designed to provide users with a real-time understanding of the availability of parking spots outside of the Lehman College of Engineering building on the campus of Embry-Rddle Aeronautical University. Click any of the elements below to learn more about the topic.

  • Topic: Show Controls
    Left click anywhere on the screen to show or hide the control panel.

    You can:
    - View the current time
    - View the number of spots available
    - Activate the prediction window
    - Open this help panel again

  • Topic: Switch Views
    Right click anywhere on the screen show the cartoon rendering of the parking lot, or return to the live view of the parking lot.

    You can:
    - View individual parking spots clearly
    - Determine occupany based car image

  • Topic: Prediction Window

    Open the prediction window to view the possibility of an open parking spot based on historical data.

    1. Click the "Predict" button on the control panel
    2. Enter in a date (via the "Date" panel) and a time (via the "Time" panel)
    3. Press "Go" and receive a prediction of whether or not parking will be available on your requested time and data.
